# Matei Zaharia

## Background

Matei Zaharia is a professor at Stanford University and co-founder of Databricks (Apache Spark). He co-authored the original ColBERT paper with Omar Khattab, contributing to the foundational work on late-interaction retrieval.

## Key Work

- **ColBERT** (SIGIR 2020) — co-author with [[Omar Khattab]]
- Apache Spark — distributed computing framework
- Databricks — data and AI platform
