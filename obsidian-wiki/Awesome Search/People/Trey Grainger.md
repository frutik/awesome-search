# Trey Grainger

## Background

Trey Grainger is the author of "AI-Powered Search" (Manning Publications) and a leading practitioner in search and knowledge graph integration. He originated the concept of **Wormhole Vectors** and the **Semantic Knowledge Graph (SKG)** for bridging vector spaces.

## Key Contributions

- **[[Wormhole Vectors]]** — using document sets to translate between sparse and dense vector spaces
- **Semantic Knowledge Graph** — statistical significant term analysis for vector space traversal
- "AI-Powered Search" book — comprehensive guide to modern search systems
