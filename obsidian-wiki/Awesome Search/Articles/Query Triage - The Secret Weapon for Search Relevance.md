# Query Triage: The Secret Weapon for Search Relevance

**Source:** https://jamesrubinstein.medium.com/query-triage-the-secret-weapon-for-search-relevance-1a02cdd297ed
**Author:** [[James Rubinstein]]

## Summary

A structured meeting process that brings cross-functional teams together to analyze poorly-performing search queries. Merges human qualitative insight with metrics-driven investigation to maximize relevance improvement ROI.

## The Triage Workflow

1. **Assembly** — gather PM, engineering, data science, UX, customer experience
2. **Investigation** — each member examines assigned queries (what went wrong and why)
3. **Classification** — problems categorized with a shared vocabulary
4. **Discussion** — cross-functional insight surfaces root causes
5. **Documentation** — problems and solutions recorded
6. **Verification** — periodically revisit queries to confirm improvements shipped

## Query Sources

- Search logs with low engagement
- Human relevance evaluations — see [[Judgment Lists]]
- Customer complaints
- Employee experiences

## Why It Works

No single expert understands all dimensions of a query failure:
- Data scientists see metric anomalies
- UX researchers understand user mental models
- Engineers know system constraints
- PMs know business priorities

Combined: identifies high-impact, feasible solutions faster.

## Key Insight

> "Triage principle: focus limited resources on the most solvable, impactful problems."
