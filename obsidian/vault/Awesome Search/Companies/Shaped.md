# Shaped

Recommendation and ranking platform (shaped.ai). Exposes ranking models like [[LambdaMART]] through a policy-config system (e.g. a [[LightGBM]] scoring policy with `objective: lambdarank` on top of a two-tower retriever). Publishes practitioner content on [[Learning to Rank]].
