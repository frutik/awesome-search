# Enterprise Knowledge LLC

Consulting firm specializing in **knowledge management, taxonomy/ontology design, and enterprise search & findability**. EK approaches search as a human-centered design problem — aligning content structure, metadata, and the search experience with how people in an organization actually look for information.

Their search-UX work emphasizes that enterprise findability fails more often from poor information architecture and taxonomy than from the retrieval engine itself.

## Notable Themes

- Human-centered approach to search design
- Taxonomy and metadata as the backbone of findability
- Integrating [[Knowledge Graph Search|search and knowledge graphs]]
