# LinkedIn

Professional social network (>1 billion members). Operates large-scale recommendation and search systems; its engineering blog details production [[Multi-Stage Ranking]] architectures.

## Search & Recommendations Engineering
- **People You May Know (PYMK)** — multi-stage funnel (L0 candidate generation → L1 light ranker → L2 rich ranker → fairness/diversity re-ranker) scoring >1B candidates per member.
