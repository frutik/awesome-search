# Jina AI

Berlin-based AI company focused on multimodal embeddings and neural search infrastructure. Known for the Jina Embeddings model family and search-oriented developer tools.

## Relevant Products

- **Jina Embeddings** (`jina-embeddings-v*`) — embedding models for semantic search; used in vector search pipelines (e.g., `jina-embeddings-v5-text-small`)
- **jina-grep-cli** — CLI tool for semantic search over local files; a semantic alternative to `grep` for agentic search contexts
  - Repo: https://github.com/jina-ai/jina-grep-cli
  - Usage: `jina-grep -r --top-k 10 "regulatory constraints" /data/`
