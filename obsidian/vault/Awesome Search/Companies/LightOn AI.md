# LightOn AI

## About

French AI company specializing in large language models and retrieval systems. Known for research and production tools in multi-vector (late interaction) retrieval.

## Tools / Projects

- **PyLate** — open-source library for training and serving late interaction models (ColBERT)
- **NextPlaid / FastPlaid** — production-grade engines for multi-vector retrieval
- **GTE-ModernColBERT** — state-of-the-art ColBERT model based on ModernBERT
