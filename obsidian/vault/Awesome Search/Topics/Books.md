# Books

Key books on search, information retrieval, NLP, and related fields. Organized by audience and focus area.

---

## Foundational IR

| Title | Authors | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *Introduction to Information Retrieval* | Manning, Raghavan, Schütze | Free online; covers TF-IDF, BM25, vector models |
| *Search Engines: Information Retrieval in Practice* | Croft, Metzler, Strohman | Textbook, covers indexing to ranking |
| *Information Retrieval: Implementing and Evaluating Search Engines* | Büttcher, Clarke, Cormack | Deep on implementation |

---

## Applied / Practitioner

| Title | Authors | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *Relevant Search* | Turnbull, Berryman | Elasticsearch-focused; mental models for relevance |
| *AI-Powered Search* | Trey Grainger et al. | Neural/semantic search with Solr/Opensearch |
| *Search Patterns* | Peter Morville, Jeffery Callender | UX patterns for search interfaces |
| *Elasticsearch: The Definitive Guide* | Clinton Gormley, Zachary Tong | Free online; Elasticsearch internals |

---

## Machine Learning for Search

| Title | Authors | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *Learning to Rank for Information Retrieval* | Liu | LTR theory and practice |
| *Neural Information Retrieval* | various | Deep learning models for IR |
