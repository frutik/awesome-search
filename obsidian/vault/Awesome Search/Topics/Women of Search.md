# Women of Search

**Women of Search (WoS)** is an international community dedicated to empowering and celebrating women working in the search field. Its mission is to provide *"a network and platform for technical growth, mentorship, and knowledge sharing"* for women in search, information retrieval, and adjacent disciplines.

🔗 https://www.women-of-search.org/

---

## History

Women of Search was founded in **May 2021** by **[[Audrey Lorberfeld]]**, a librarian-turned-software engineer, out of a desire to form meaningful connections with women in the search and information retrieval field. It has since grown into an international community with a core leadership team and a roster of speakers and mentors.

## Focus Areas

The community spans the breadth of modern search practice:

- Search technology and [[Information Retrieval]]
- [[Vector Search]] and semantic retrieval
- [[Recommender Systems|Recommendation systems]]
- Search product management
- Career growth, mentorship, and knowledge sharing

## Activities

- **Talks at industry conferences** — most notably the [[Events and Conferences|Haystack Conference]] (US and EU editions), where WoS members present technical sessions.
- **Maven workshops** on specialized topics such as AI-powered code search.
- **Mentorship program** — pairing women in the field with experienced practitioners for career and technical growth.
- **Blog and resources** — written knowledge sharing and a curated resource list.
- **YouTube talks** — recorded conference and community sessions spanning 2022–2025.
