# Geoffrey Hinton

Pioneer of deep learning; Turing Award winner (2018). Co-created [[t-SNE]] with [[Laurens van der Maaten]] (2008). Known for foundational work on backpropagation, Boltzmann machines, and deep neural networks.

## Key Contributions

- [[t-SNE]] — co-author with van der Maaten (JMLR 2008)
