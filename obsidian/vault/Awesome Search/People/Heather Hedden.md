# Heather Hedden

Taxonomist and author of **The Accidental Taxonomist** (book and [blog](https://accidental-taxonomist.blogspot.com)), a long-running reference on practical taxonomy and metadata design. Her work directly informs **faceted search**: how many facets a taxonomy should expose, when a taxonomy should *not* be hierarchical, and how to customize facets for a given domain.

## Notable Writing

- *How Many Facets Should a Taxonomy Have* — guidance on facet count vs. usability
- *When a Taxonomy Should Not Be Hierarchical* — flat vs. tree structures
- *Customizing Taxonomy Facets* — tailoring facets to content and users
