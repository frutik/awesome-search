# Andrew McCallum

Machine-learning / NLP researcher (UMass Amherst, IESL) recurring across the [[Region-Based Representation|region-embedding]] line in this vault:

- **Gaussian Embedding** (Vilnis & McCallum, ICLR 2015) — words as Gaussians. See [[Gaussian Embedding]].
- **[[Answering Compositional Queries with Set-Theoretic Embeddings]]** (Dasgupta, McCallum, Rendle, Zhang, 2023) — [[Box Embedding|box embeddings]] for [[Compositional Queries|compositional]] item–attribute queries.

His group is associated with much of the probabilistic box-embedding research that produced [[Box Embedding]] and [[Word2Box]].
