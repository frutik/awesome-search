# Daniel Doro

Search engineering leader at **Spotify**. Co-author (with Hugo Galvão) of Spotify's search organization evolution article.

## Key Contributions

- **Search as a Platform**: Co-led Spotify's search reorganization. Identified that growing team headcount without changing structure creates coordination overhead that cancels productivity gains. Platform model separates product experimentation from infrastructure maintenance.
