# Audrey Lorberfeld

A **librarian-turned-software engineer** working in search and information retrieval. In **May 2021** she founded **[[Women of Search]]**, an international community for women in the search field, out of a desire to form meaningful connections with women in search and IR.

## Known For
- Founder of [[Women of Search]]
- Talks on **AI-Driven Information Retrieval**
- Bridging library science and software engineering in search practice
