# Udi Manber

Search leader who has led search at Amazon, Yahoo, Google, and YouTube. Quoted for naming a persistent misperception that undermines search investment:

> "Many people believe that search is essentially a solved problem, and it just works."

This belief — that search "just works" — is part of why teams under-invest in defining relevance and evaluation before reaching for sophisticated solutions.
