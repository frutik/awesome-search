# Steffen Rendle

Researcher known for foundational **recommender-systems** work (factorization machines, BPR — Bayesian Personalized Ranking). Co-author of [[Answering Compositional Queries with Set-Theoretic Embeddings]], which applies [[Box Embedding|box embeddings]] to item–attribute [[Compositional Queries|compositional queries]] relevant to recommendation and faceted browsing.
