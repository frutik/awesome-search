# Tomas Mikolov

Researcher best known for **[[Word2Vec]]** (Mikolov et al., 2013) — the CBOW and skip-gram word-embedding architectures with negative sampling that popularized dense word vectors and the `king − man + woman ≈ queen` embedding-algebra observation. Word2Vec's CBOW scheme is reused directly by [[Word2Box]], and its skip-gram/negative-sampling recipe underpins many learned search embeddings across this vault (Query2Vec, listing embeddings, synonym mining).
