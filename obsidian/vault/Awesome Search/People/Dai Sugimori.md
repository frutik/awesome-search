# Dai Sugimori

Software Engineer and technical writer at **Elastic**. Author of documentation on sparse vector embeddings in Elasticsearch, including the new Elasticsearch 8.17 support for uploading custom sparse models.

## Key Contributions

- **Sparse vs Dense Vectors**: Wrote accessible comparison of dense and sparse vector embeddings, explaining why sparse vectors (ELSER, SPLADE) offer zero-shot generalization and resource efficiency advantages for domains without sufficient fine-tuning data.
- **Custom Sparse Model Upload**: Documented Elasticsearch 8.17's new Eland CLI support for uploading Hugging Face sparse models (SPLADE v3, Japanese SPLADE) as alternatives to ELSER.
