# Adrien Grand

Principal engineer at [[Elastic]], core Apache Lucene committer. Implemented [[Block-Max WAND]] in Lucene 8.0 and Elasticsearch 7.0 (2019) — described as one of the largest performance improvements in Lucene's history. Also drove the WAND (Weak AND) optimization that preceded it.
