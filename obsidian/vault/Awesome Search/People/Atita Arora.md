# Atita Arora

Search and relevance practitioner who consults across search and retrieval (and increasingly AI-boosted features) in e-commerce, health, media, finance, legal, enterprise, government, geospatial, and marketplace systems. Writes the [Unscripted AI](https://unscriptedai.substack.com/) Substack.

Her central thesis: most search problems are not unique but instances of a small number of recurring patterns, and the highest-leverage step is **correctly diagnosing which pattern you are in before choosing a solution** — see [[Search Problem Archetypes]].
