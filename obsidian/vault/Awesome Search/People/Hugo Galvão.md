# Hugo Galvão

Search engineering leader at **Spotify**. Co-author (with Daniel Doro) of Spotify's search organization evolution article.

## Key Contributions

- **Search as a Platform**: Led Spotify's transition from problem-oriented and stack-oriented team structures to a **platform model** — splitting search into "core search" (end-user metrics) and "search platform" (developer happiness + SLOs). Identified system centrality and congestion as quantitative signals for when team structure needs to change.
